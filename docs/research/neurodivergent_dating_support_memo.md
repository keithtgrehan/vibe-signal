# Neurodivergent Dating Support Memo

Vibe neurodivergent support means clarity, agency, overload reduction, explicit asks, repair messages, and boundary-safe communication.

The product must never infer neurodivergence from text. It must not say a user or another person is autistic, has ADHD, is neurodivergent, or has a mental-health condition.

## Supported Product Behaviors

- Shorten dense messages.
- Flag when an ask is not explicit.
- Offer more direct wording.
- Offer lower-pressure wording.
- Provide literal summaries that avoid feeling guesses.
- Suggest repair messages that preserve agency.

## Non-Goals

- No diagnosis.
- No hidden-intent claims.
- No attraction prediction.
- No attachment-style inference.
- No emotional-manipulation tactics.
- No coercive dating advice.
