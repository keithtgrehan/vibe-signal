# Known Limitations - 2026-06-09

Status: Draft for tester instructions; pending owner approval.

Tester-facing limitations:

- Vibe Signal reviews observable wording cues only.
- It cannot determine intent, attraction, deception, diagnosis, neurotype, attachment style, emotional truth, or relationship outcomes.
- Use synthetic examples first.
- Only submit messages you have permission to analyze.
- Do not submit sensitive personal data, secrets, workplace confidential content, medical/legal/financial content, or third-party private messages without permission.
- Closed beta feedback is metadata-only.
- Real-device QA must use synthetic fixtures and screenshots only.
- The app is not a therapy, legal, medical, financial, or safety decision tool.
