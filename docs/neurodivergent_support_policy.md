# Neurodivergent Communication Support Policy

Vibe Engine may support clearer communication for users who want more explicit, lower-pressure, and easier-to-scan messages. It must never infer that a user or another person is autistic, has ADHD, is neurodivergent, or has any mental-health condition.

## Allowed Support

- Reduce overload by shortening dense text.
- Make an ask more explicit.
- Offer a more direct version.
- Offer a lower-pressure version.
- Summarize literal content without guessing feelings.
- Draft repair messages that preserve agency and boundaries.

## Required Framing

- "This may be easier to read if shortened."
- "The ask is not explicit."
- "A more direct version could be..."
- "A lower-pressure version could be..."
- "This keeps the message clearer without guessing how the other person feels."

## Disallowed Support

- No diagnosis or identity inference.
- No partner mind-reading.
- No "make them like you" framing.
- No coercive or persuasion tactics.
- No advice to exploit vulnerability.
- No true-emotion, deception, attraction, attachment-style, or protected-trait claims.
