## App Name

Vibe Signal

## Subtitle

Communication pattern review

## Beta Description

Vibe Signal helps closed-beta testers review observable wording patterns in synthetic or permissioned messages. It highlights clarity, ambiguity, pressure, reassurance, cognitive load, unanswered asks, boundary pressure, escalation risk, and repair opportunities.

Vibe Signal does not read minds, predict attraction, decide whether someone is lying, diagnose anyone, provide therapy, or tell users how to influence another person. Results are evidence-backed communication-support prompts with limits and suggested next steps.

## Beta Notes For Testers

- Use synthetic examples first.
- Submit only messages you have permission to analyze.
- Do not submit names, phone numbers, addresses, minors' content, medical/legal/financial/workplace-sensitive content, or highly sensitive third-party content.
- Report confusing, unsafe, or overconfident output with metadata only. Do not include private message text in reports.
