# Beta Tester Instructions

Status: draft closed-beta instructions. Do not send until tester invites are approved.

## Before Testing

- Start with the synthetic example.
- Use only messages you have permission to analyze.
- Do not submit names, phone numbers, addresses, minors' content, medical/legal/financial/workplace-sensitive content, or highly sensitive third-party content.

## What To Check

- The app explains what it can and cannot tell you before analysis.
- Results include evidence phrases, limits, and safe next steps.
- Short or unclear text returns a low-signal state.
- Feedback can be submitted once per result without exposing private message content.
- Legal and support links are visible.

## What Not To Use It For

Do not use Vibe Signal to decide private feelings, hidden motives, deception, diagnosis, relationship-style labels, whether someone will reply, or how to pressure someone.

## Reporting Issues

Send metadata only:

- build label
- device model and OS
- screen name
- approximate time
- what went wrong

Do not include copied private messages, screenshots with private text, request bodies, response bodies, secrets, or tester identity details.
