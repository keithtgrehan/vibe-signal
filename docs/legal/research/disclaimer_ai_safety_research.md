# Disclaimer / AI Safety Research

Status: draft_requires_legal_review

This is research grounding for Vibe Signal disclaimer and AI safety copy. It is not legal approval and does not claim that any AI law or standard requirements are satisfied.

## Product Safety Boundary

- Vibe Signal is communication support only.
- It provides wording-based suggestions only.
- It uses observable text evidence only.
- It should show evidence before interpretation and keep limits visible.
- It should use low-signal fallback language when there is not enough context.
- It should recommend safe, respectful, non-coercive communication.

## Cannot-Infer Statement

Use this exact public statement:

> Vibe Signal does not know intent, attraction, truthfulness, diagnosis, or outcomes.

## Professional And Safety Limits

- Not therapy.
- Not medical advice.
- Not legal advice.
- Not financial advice.
- Not emergency/crisis support.
- Not a substitute for human judgment, user context, consent, or respectful communication.
- If there is urgent risk or a safety situation, users should contact appropriate emergency or local support services.

## Unsafe Framing To Avoid

- No hidden-intent detection.
- No attraction prediction.
- No truthfulness/deception certainty.
- No cheating detection.
- No relationship-outcome prediction.
- No diagnosis or therapy framing.
- No emotion-recognition positioning.
- No manipulation advice.
- No "make them respond" framing.
- No invented popularity or urgency.

## Unresolved Legal-Review Questions

- Whether additional AI-specific disclosures are needed for the launch geography.
- Whether the app should include age-gating or stronger minors restrictions.
- Whether provider-specific model disclosures are needed if external providers are enabled.
- Whether crisis-support routing needs jurisdiction-specific wording.
