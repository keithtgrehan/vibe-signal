## Terms Topics To Cover

- Closed beta / early public beta status.
- User permission requirement: "Only submit text you have permission to analyze."
- Permitted use: communication clarity, reflection, and drafting safer replies.
- Prohibited use:
  - stalking
  - harassment
  - coercion
  - manipulation
  - trying to make someone respond
  - hidden-intent, attraction, truthfulness, deception, or relationship-outcome certainty claims
  - workplace/education emotional assessment
  - minors/teen romantic analysis
  - uploading content the user lacks rights or permission to use
- No professional advice.
- No therapy, medical, legal, or financial advice.
- No emergency support.
- Service availability / no warranty language requiring legal review.
- Account features are not currently implemented.
- Payment features are not currently implemented.

## Product-Boundary Wording

Use communication-support framing:

- Vibe Signal reviews observable wording.
- Outputs are suggestions and limits, not truth claims.
- Vibe Signal does not know intent, attraction, truthfulness, diagnosis, or outcomes.
- Users should apply human judgment and avoid coercive communication.

Avoid launch or legal-readiness claims:

- Do not say the GDPR requirements are satisfied.
- Do not say the EU AI Act requirements are satisfied.
- Do not say legal review is complete.
